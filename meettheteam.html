Meet the Current Team

Engineered From the Group Up

DermaVision Technologies began as a BME undergraduate-led project under the
mentorship of Dr. Elliot Botvinick. Starting from scratch, inspired students designed a
proof-of-concept device, created skin models to mimick bruising, and established basic
image processing techniques for analyzing acquired data.

Our diverse team is working diligently towards a minimum viable product, and continue
gathering data in conjunction with the UCI Trauma Center. We are looking forward
to displaying more of our progress in the near future!
